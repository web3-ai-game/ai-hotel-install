任务: 配置智能AI助手
Task: Configure Intelligent AI Assistant

描述:
为酒店系统添加一个智能AI助手，能够用自然语言与客人交互，处理常见查询和请求。

Description:
Add an intelligent AI assistant to the hotel system that can interact with guests in natural language and handle common queries and requests.

功能需求 (Feature Requirements):

1. 多语言支持 (Multi-language Support)
   - 中文 (Chinese)
   - 英文 (English)
   - 自动语言检测 (Automatic language detection)

2. 对话能力 (Conversation Capabilities)
   - 房间查询（价格、可用性、设施）
   - Room inquiries (price, availability, amenities)
   - 预订管理（创建、查看、修改、取消）
   - Reservation management (create, view, modify, cancel)
   - 酒店服务信息（餐厅、健身房、Spa等）
   - Hotel services info (restaurant, gym, spa, etc.)
   - 本地旅游建议
   - Local tourism recommendations

3. 智能功能 (Intelligent Features)
   - 理解用户意图
   - Understand user intent
   - 上下文记忆（记住对话历史）
   - Context memory (remember conversation history)
   - 个性化推荐
   - Personalized recommendations
   - 24/7 可用
   - 24/7 availability

预期结果 (Expected Outcomes):
- ✅ AI助手可以准确理解90%以上的常见查询
- ✅ 响应时间 < 2秒
- ✅ 支持同时处理多个对话会话
- ✅ 友好的用户体验
- ✅ 错误时能够优雅降级（转接人工客服）

Expected Outcomes:
- ✅ AI assistant can accurately understand 90%+ of common queries
- ✅ Response time < 2 seconds
- ✅ Support concurrent conversation sessions
- ✅ Friendly user experience
- ✅ Graceful degradation on errors (transfer to human support)

技术集成 (Technical Integration):
- 使用主流AI/LLM服务（OpenAI, Claude, 本地模型等）
- Use mainstream AI/LLM services (OpenAI, Claude, local models, etc.)
- WebSocket或轮询支持实时对话
- WebSocket or polling for real-time conversation
- 对话历史存储
- Conversation history storage

依赖 (Dependencies):
- setup-hotel-system.txt

优先级 (Priority): 高 (High)
